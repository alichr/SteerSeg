Overview

SteerSeg steers attention at its source by training a small set of learnable soft prompts on a frozen LVLM, paired with reasoning-guided Chain-of-Thought. The result: spatially concentrated attention maps that translate into accurate point prompts for reasoning video segmentation.

Highlights

What makes SteerSeg work.

Steer at the source

Reshape attention through input-level conditioning, instead of post-hoc patching of attention maps.

CoT-guided reasoning

Chain-of-Thought attributes disambiguate similar objects and direct attention to the correct instance.

Frozen model, learned prompts

The LVLM and segmentation model stay frozen. Only a small set of soft prompts is trained — preserving pretrained reasoning while adapting grounding.

Strong generalization

Trained only on Ref-YouTube-VOS, yet generalizes across diverse referring & reasoning video segmentation benchmarks.
